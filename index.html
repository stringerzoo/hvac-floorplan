🔧 HVAC Floorplan Help

🏢 Navigation

Use the floor buttons at the top to switch between building levels. The overlay toggle shows/hides the colored HVAC zone information.

🔍 Zoom & Pan

Use the zoom controls or mouse wheel to zoom in for detail view. When zoomed in, click and drag to pan around the floor plan.

🔎 Search

Type room names, zones, or equipment in the search box to quickly jump to relevant floors with overlay enabled.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

L Lower Level 1 First Floor 2 Second Floor 3 Third Floor Space Toggle HVAC Overlay = Zoom In - Zoom Out 0 Reset Zoom

💡 Tips

Yellow zones represent office scheduling areas (consistent across floors).
Other colors vary by floor - refer to the overlay text for specific zone details.
Mobile users: Tap and drag to pan when zoomed in.
